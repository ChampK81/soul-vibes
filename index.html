✦ Yoga Therapy & Wellness

Gentle, grounded support for the nervous system.

Soul Vibes offers slow, therapeutic yoga for people experiencing stress, disconnection, or overwhelm. This work emphasises body awareness, internal safety, and practical tools for daily life, and may complement talk therapy or support those who are curious about a more body-based approach.

This work is practical, grounded, and paced with care — meeting you where you are, without pressure to fix or change yourself.

Yoga therapy is more than a yoga class.

Yoga therapy uses yoga-based tools in a personalized, therapeutic way.

It can support self-awareness, regulation, resilience, and overall well-being. The work is adapted to the individual rather than asking the person to fit the practice.

How is it different from a general yoga class?

  • It is tailored to the person, not taught to a room in the same way
  • It focuses on therapeutic goals rather than performance
  • It can include stillness, awareness, breath, movement, and reflection
  • Choice, pacing, and accessibility are central
Who is yoga therapy for?

Yoga therapy can support people moving through stress, burnout, overwhelm, mental load, disconnection, transitions, or a desire to feel more resourced in daily life.

What kinds of practices are used?

Sessions can include grounding, breathing practices, gentle movement, awareness-based practices, nervous system education, stillness, rest, and simple tools to carry into everyday life.

Do I need yoga experience?

No. You do not need yoga experience, flexibility, or any particular kind of body. The work is meant to meet you where you are and can be adapted accordingly.

A calmer way in.

This work is not about pushing or performing. It is about learning how to listen, respond, and relate to your body in a way that feels sustainable and supportive over time.
