This is a space for all bodies, all nervous systems.

Accessible by design

The practice is always adapted to you — not the other way around. No experience, no particular body type, no baseline level of wellness is required to begin.

Grounded in relationship

The therapeutic relationship matters. Sessions are conversational, slow enough to be real, and shaped by what is actually present — not a pre-determined script.

Non-performative

This work is not about achieving or improving in a measurable way. It is about learning to listen, respond, and relate to yourself with more ease and less force.
