What to expect in a session

Sessions typically begin with a conversation about how you are arriving — what is present, what feels heavy, or what you are hoping to tend to. From there, the practice is shaped around you.

This might include breath work, gentle movement, stillness, grounding practices, awareness techniques, or nervous system education. Often it is a mix. You are always in choice.

Common reasons people seek support

  • Chronic stress, burnout, or overwhelm
  • Difficulty resting or slowing down
  • Feeling disconnected from the body
  • Navigating a life transition or change
  • Wanting to build a sustainable self-care practice
  • Supporting mental health alongside other care
  • General desire to feel more grounded and resourced
